AI PING PONG GAME











Developed By : Nitya H. Amin

Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from your laptop
  3. This game is like ping pong but you use your right hand, which you need to move up and or down depending on where the ball is
  4. Now press play and move back to your previous position
  5. The red paddel is yours, that will move depedning on where your right hand is
  6. This is a mini project so I hope you like it!
